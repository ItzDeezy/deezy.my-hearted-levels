# My Hearted Levels
<cg>Quickly favorite and filter your created levels</c>

<cr>My Hearted Levels</c> lets you <cr>favorite</c> your created levels and instantly <cg>filter</c> them in the My Levels tab, making it easier to access the levels you're actively working on without searching or scrolling through multiple pages.

## Features
• <cr>Favorite</c> any level directly from the Create Tab
• <cg>Filter</c> the list to show only your favorited levels, with support for multiple pages

## Usage
• Click the <cr>heart button</c> next to a level to favorite it  
• Use the <cg>heart filter</c> in the My Levels tab to display only your favorites
